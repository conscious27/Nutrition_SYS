Ingredients:

2 cups wheat flour
1/2 cup ghee
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp ajwain seeds
1 tsp coriander seeds
1 tsp fennel seeds
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup water, or as needed
Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, add wheat flour, cumin seeds, ajwain seeds, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, and salt. Mix well.
2. Add ghee and mix with your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
3. Gradually add water and knead the mixture into a firm dough.
4. Divide the dough into equal portions and shape them into balls.
5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
6. Place the balls on a baking sheet and bake for 30-40 minutes or until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside.
7. Serve hot with dal and chutney.
