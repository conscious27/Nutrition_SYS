Ingredients:

1 cup cooked rice
1 cup yogurt
1/4 cup milk
Salt to taste
1 tbsp oil
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp urad dal
1/2 tsp chana dal
1-2 dried red chilies
2-3 curry leaves
1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
Instructions:

1. In a large mixing bowl, add cooked rice and mash it well using a spatula or your hands.
2. Add yogurt and milk to the mashed rice and mix it well. Ensure that there are no lumps.
3. Add salt to taste and mix it well.
4. In a pan, heat oil on medium flame.
5. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
6. Add cumin seeds, urad dal, chana dal, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Saute for a minute or until the dals turn golden brown.
7. Pour the tempering over the curd rice mixture and mix it well.
8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve chilled.
