Ingredients:

200g paneer
2 medium-sized onions, chopped
2 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp red chilli powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp garam masala powder
1/2 tsp kasuri methi
1/4 cup cream
Salt to taste
1 tbsp butter
1 tbsp oil
1/4 cup water
Instructions:

1. Heat 1 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
6. Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down.
8. Once cooled, grind the mixture to a smooth paste.
9. Strain the paste and transfer it back to the pan.
10. Add 1/4 cup water and bring the mixture to a boil.
11. Add paneer cubes, garam masala powder, and kasuri methi. 12. Mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes.
13. Add cream and mix well.
14. Turn off the heat and garnish with coriander leaves.
15. Serve hot with naan or rice.
