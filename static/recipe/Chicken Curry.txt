Ingredients:

1 lb chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
1 tbsp ginger paste
1 tbsp garlic paste
1 tsp cumin powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste
1 cup tomato puree
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup water
Cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
Instructions:

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. 2. Add the chopped onions and cook until they are translucent.
3. Add the ginger and garlic paste and cook for a few more minutes until fragrant.
4. Add the cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder to the pan. Cook for a minute or so until the spices are toasted and fragrant.
5. Add the chicken to the pan and season with salt. Cook until the chicken is browned on all sides.
6. Add the tomato puree and yogurt to the pan. Stir to combine.
7. Add the water to the pan and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
8. Add the garam masala and stir to combine. Cook for a few more minutes until the flavors have melded together.
9. Serve the chicken curry hot, garnished with cilantro.
