Ingredients:

1 cup urad dal (black gram)
1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
1 tsp grated ginger
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp black pepper
Salt to taste
Oil for frying
Instructions:

1. Soak urad dal in water for at least 4-5 hours.
2. Drain the water and grind the urad dal in a food processor until it turns into a smooth batter. Add a little water if necessary.
3. Add chopped green chilies, grated ginger, cumin seeds, black pepper, and salt to the batter. Mix well.
4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat.
5. Take a small amount of batter and shape it into a small ball. Flatten it with your fingers to form a round shape with a hole in the center.
6. Slowly drop the vada into the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
7. Drain the excess oil on a paper towel.
8. Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.
