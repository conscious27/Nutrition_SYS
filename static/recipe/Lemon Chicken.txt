Ingredients:

500g boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup cornstarch
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup chicken broth
Juice of 1 lemon
Zest of 1 lemon
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon honey
Lemon wedges for serving
Chopped parsley for garnish
Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, salt, and pepper.
2. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and toss to coat well.
Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through.
4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
5. Add the garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
6. Pour in the chicken broth and lemon juice, scraping the bottom of the skillet to release any browned bits.
7. Add the lemon zest, oregano, and honey to the skillet and stir to combine.
8. Allow the sauce to simmer for a few minutes until it thickens.
9. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss to coat well with the sauce.
10. Cook for another minute or two until the chicken is heated through.
11. Serve the lemon chicken with lemon wedges and chopped parsley.
