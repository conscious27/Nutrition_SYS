Ingredients:

2 cups of cooked basmati rice
2 eggs
1 small onion, diced
1/2 cup of frozen peas
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 tablespoons of oil
2 tablespoons of soy sauce
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a wok or large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
2. Add the onion and garlic, and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
3. Add the frozen peas and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes.
Push the onion and peas to one side of the wok and crack the eggs into the empty space.
4. Scramble the eggs with a spatula until they are cooked through, then mix them with the onion and peas.
5. Add the cooked rice to the wok and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
6. Drizzle the soy sauce over the rice and mix well.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Serve hot and enjoy!
