Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice
1 onion, sliced
1 tomato, chopped
1/2 inch ginger, grated
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 bay leaf
2 cloves
2 green cardamoms
1 cinnamon stick
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
2 cups water or vegetable broth
2 tbsp oil
Salt to taste
Instructions:

1. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add the cloves, green cardamoms, bay leaf, and cinnamon stick. Fry for a few seconds.
3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
4. Add ginger, garlic, and fry for a minute.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft.
6. Add soaked rice, salt, and all the spices (cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder). Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Add 2 cups of water or vegetable broth. Mix well and bring it to a boil.
8. Reduce heat, cover the pan and let it cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked.
9. Once the rice is cooked, turn off the heat and let it rest for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
