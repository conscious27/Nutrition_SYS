Ingredients:

200g paneer (cottage cheese)
2 tbsp oil
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 onion, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, finely chopped
2 green chillies, finely chopped
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp red chilli powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
Instructions:

1. Crumble the paneer and keep it aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
3. Add finely chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
4. Add finely chopped tomatoes and green chillies. Cook until the tomatoes turn soft and mushy.
5 .Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chilli powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
6. Add crumbled paneer and mix well with the masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Finally, add chopped coriander leaves and mix well.
8. Serve hot with chapati, paratha or bread.
