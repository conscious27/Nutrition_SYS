Ingredients:

2 cups puffed rice
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 small tomato, finely chopped
1 small potato, boiled and diced
1/4 cup roasted peanuts
1/4 cup sev (crunchy chickpea noodles)
1 green chili, finely chopped
1 tbsp tamarind paste
1 tbsp green chutney
1/2 tsp chaat masala
Salt to taste
Coriander leaves, for garnish
Lemon wedges, for serving
Instructions:

1 .In a large mixing bowl, add puffed rice, chopped onion, tomato, and boiled potato.
2. Add roasted peanuts and sev to the bowl.
3. Add chopped green chili, tamarind paste, green chutney, chaat masala, and salt to taste. Mix well.
4. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve immediately with lemon wedges.
