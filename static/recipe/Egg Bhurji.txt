4 eggs
1 onion, finely chopped
1 tomato, finely chopped
1 green chili, finely chopped
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
Salt, to taste
2 tbsp oil
Coriander leaves, finely chopped

Instructions:

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until the yolks and whites are well combined.
2. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
3. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until it turns translucent.
4. Add the chopped tomato and green chili and cook for 3-4 minutes until the tomatoes become soft.
5. Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt to the pan and mix well.
6. Pour the whisked eggs into the pan and stir gently until the eggs are cooked.
7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
8. Serve hot with roti or bread.
