Ingredients:

1 cup sabudana (sago/tapioca pearls)
2 tbsp oil
1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
1 potato, peeled and chopped into small cubes
1/2 cup roasted peanuts, coarsely ground
2-3 green chilies, chopped
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp lemon juice
Salt to taste
Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
Instructions:

1. Wash sabudana in water until it becomes clear. Soak 2. sabudana in 1.5 cups of water for 2-3 hours.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Fry for a few seconds.
3. Add chopped potatoes and fry until they are soft and cooked.
4. Add chopped green chilies and fry for a minute.
5. Add soaked sabudana, salt, sugar, and ground peanuts. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
6. Add lemon juice and mix well.
7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
