Ingredients:

2 ripe mangoes, peeled and chopped
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 green chili, seeded and chopped
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup water
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp ground coriander
Salt to taste

Instructions:

1. In a blender or food processor, combine the chopped mangoes, onion, cilantro, and green chili. Blend until smooth.
2. In a small saucepan, combine the sugar, water, cumin, coriander, and salt. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar has dissolved.
3. Add the mango mixture to the saucepan and stir to combine.
4. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for about 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chutney has thickened to your desired consistency.
5. Remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.
6. Transfer the chutney to an airtight container and refrigerate until ready to serve.
