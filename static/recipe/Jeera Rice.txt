Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice
1 tbsp cumin seeds (jeera)
1 onion, sliced
2 cloves
2 green cardamoms
1 cinnamon stick
2 cups water
2 tbsp oil
Salt to taste
Instructions:

1. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add the cloves, green cardamoms, and cinnamon stick. Fry for a few seconds.
3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
4. Add cumin seeds and fry for a minute.
5. Add soaked rice, salt, and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
6. Add 2 cups of water. Mix well and bring it to a boil.
7. Reduce heat, cover the pan and let it cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked.
8. Once the rice is cooked, turn off the heat and let it rest for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
