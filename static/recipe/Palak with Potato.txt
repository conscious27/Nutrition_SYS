Ingredients:

2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 bunch of spinach leaves, washed and chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1-inch piece of ginger, grated
1 green chili, chopped
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
Salt to taste
2 tbsp oil
Instructions:

1. In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
2. Add chopped onions, ginger, garlic, and green chili. Saute until onions turn translucent.
3. Add cubed potatoes and mix well. Add turmeric powder and salt. Stir to combine.
4. Cover the pan with a lid and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and cooked through.
5. Add chopped spinach leaves and mix well. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the spinach is wilted and cooked.
6. Add coriander powder and red chili powder. Mix well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
7. Serve hot with rice or roti.
