Ingredients:

200 grams paneer, cut into cubes
1 large onion, chopped
2-3 garlic cloves, minced
1 teaspoon ginger paste
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1 teaspoon garam masala
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 cup tomato puree
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 tablespoon ghee or oil
Salt to taste
Chopped cilantro for garnish
Instructions:

1. Heat ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
2. Add chopped onions, minced garlic and sauté until onions turn translucent.
3. Add ginger paste, coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder and sauté for another minute.
4. Add tomato puree, salt to taste and cook for 5-7 minutes until the mixture starts leaving oil from the sides.
5. Add paneer cubes and stir to coat them with the tomato mixture.
6. Add heavy cream and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
