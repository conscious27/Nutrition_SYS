Ingredients:

1 cup whole black lentils (sabut urad dal)
1/4 cup red kidney beans (rajma)
1 onion, finely chopped
1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
2 tomatoes, pureed
2 green chilies, slit
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1 teaspoon garam masala powder
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon oil
1/4 cup fresh cream
Salt, to taste
Water, as required
Instructions:

1. Wash and soak the black lentils and kidney beans overnight in enough water.

2. Drain the water and rinse the lentils and beans.

3. In a pressure cooker, add the lentils and beans along with 4 cups of water and pressure cook for 10-12 whistles or until they are soft and well cooked.

4. Heat a pan and add butter and oil.

5. Once the butter melts, add cumin seeds and let them crackle.

6. Add finely chopped onion and sauté until they turn golden brown.

7. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.

8. Add tomato puree and mix well. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.

9. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well.

10. Add slit green chilies and cook for a minute.

11. Add the cooked lentils and beans along with the water they were cooked in. Mix well.

12. Add salt to taste and let the mixture simmer for 15-20 minutes on low flame.

13. Add fresh cream and mix well.

14. Serve hot with rice or naan.
