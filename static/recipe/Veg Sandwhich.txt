Ingredients:

6 slices of bread
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1/2 cup shredded cabbage
1/2 cup sliced cucumbers
1/2 cup sliced tomatoes
1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
1/4 tsp chat masala
Salt and pepper to taste
Butter for spreading
Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix together shredded carrots, shredded cabbage, sliced cucumbers, sliced tomatoes, chopped coriander leaves, chat masala, salt and pepper.
2. Take two slices of bread and spread butter on one side of each slice.
3. Place 2-3 tbsp of the vegetable mixture on one slice of bread and spread it evenly.
4. Cover with the other slice of bread with the buttered side facing down.
5. Repeat with the remaining slices of bread and vegetable mixture.
6. Cut the sandwiches into halves or quarters.
7. Serve with ketchup or chutney.
