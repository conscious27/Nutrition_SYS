Ingredients:

1 cup besan (gram flour)
2 cups sour yogurt
3-4 cups water
1 tbsp ginger paste
1 tbsp garlic paste
2-3 green chillies, chopped
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
2-3 dried red chillies
1 sprig curry leaves
Salt to taste
2 tbsp oil
1 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp red chilli powder
1 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, whisk the besan and yogurt together with 2 cups of water until smooth.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dried red chillies, and asafoetida. Fry for 1-2 minutes.
3. Add ginger and garlic paste, green chillies, and curry leaves. Fry for 1-2 minutes.
4. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, and salt. Mix well and fry for 1-2 minutes.
5. Add the besan-yogurt mixture and 1-2 cups of water to the pan. Mix well and bring to a boil.
6. Lower the heat and let the kadhi simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. Adjust salt and water as needed. The kadhi should have a thick consistency.
8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice.
