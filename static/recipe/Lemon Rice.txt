Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice
2 cups water
2 tbsp oil
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 cup peanuts
1/2 cup fresh curry leaves
2-3 green chilies, chopped
1/2 lemon, juiced
Salt to taste
Instructions:

1. Rinse rice and soak in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
2. In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to boil. Add rice and stir. Cover with a lid and let it cook until the rice is fully cooked.
3. In a separate pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Fry for a few seconds.
4. Add turmeric powder, peanuts, curry leaves, and chopped green chilies. Fry until peanuts turn golden brown.
5. Add cooked rice to the pan and mix well. Squeeze the juice of half a lemon and mix well. Add salt to taste.
6. Serve hot.
