Ingredients:

1 cup dried chickpeas
1 large onion, finely chopped
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
2 green chilies, chopped
1 teaspoon ginger paste
1 teaspoon garlic paste
2 teaspoons cumin seeds
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
1/2 teaspoon garam masala
Salt to taste
2 tablespoons oil
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoons fresh coriander leaves, chopped
Instructions:

1. Wash the chickpeas thoroughly and soak them in water overnight.

2. Drain the water from the chickpeas and pressure cook them with 3 cups of water and a pinch of salt for 15-20 minutes on medium heat. Once done, keep aside.

3. In a pan, heat oil and butter together. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

4. Add chopped onions and sauté till they turn golden brown.

5. Add ginger and garlic paste, chopped green chilies, and sauté for 2-3 minutes.

6. Add chopped tomatoes and mix well.

7. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and let it cook for 5-7 minutes.

8. Add the cooked chickpeas and 1/2 cup of water. Mix well and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.

9. Once the gravy thickens, add garam masala and mix well.

10. Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves.
