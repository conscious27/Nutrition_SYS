Ingredients:

2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
1/2 cup green peas, boiled and mashed (optional)
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp garam masala powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp amchur powder (dried mango powder)
Salt, to taste
2 tbsp corn flour
Oil, for frying
Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, add the mashed potatoes and mashed green peas (if using).
2. Add cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, red chili powder, amchur powder, and salt to the mixing bowl. Mix well.
3. Add corn flour to the mixing bowl and mix well.
4. Divide the mixture into equal-sized balls and shape them into flattened rounds.
5. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
6. Once the oil is hot, gently slide the aloo tikki into the oil and fry until it becomes golden brown on both sides.
7. Drain the excess oil on a paper towel.
8. Serve hot with chutney or as a topping for chaat.
