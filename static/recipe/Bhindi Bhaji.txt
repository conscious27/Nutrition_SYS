Ingredients:

250g bhindi (okra)
2 tbsp oil
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 onion, chopped
1 green chili, chopped
1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1/4 tsp red chili powder
Salt to taste
1/4 cup water
Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
Instructions:

1. Wash and dry the bhindi. Cut off the head and tail, and slice them into pieces.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Allow them to splutter.
3. Add chopped onions, green chili, and ginger-garlic paste. Saute until onions become soft and golden brown.
4. Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a minute.
5. Add the sliced bhindi and mix well with the spices.
6. Pour in water, cover the pan with a lid, and let it cook for 10-15 minutes on low heat until bhindi becomes soft and tender.
7. Remove the lid and cook on high heat for another 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
