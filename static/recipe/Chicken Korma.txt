Ingredients:

500g chicken
1 cup onion paste
1 cup tomato puree
1/2 cup yogurt
1 tsp ginger paste
1 tsp garlic paste
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/2 cup cream
2 tbsp oil
Salt to taste
Chopped cilantro for garnish
Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onion paste and fry until golden brown.
2. Add ginger paste, garlic paste, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Fry for 1-2 minutes.
3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the mixture.
4. Add chicken and fry until it changes color.
Add yogurt and salt. Mix well and cook until chicken is tender.
5. Add garam masala and cream. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
