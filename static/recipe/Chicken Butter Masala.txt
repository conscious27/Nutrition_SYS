Ingredients:

500g boneless chicken, cut into small pieces
2 large onions, chopped
2 large tomatoes, chopped
2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp cumin powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp garam masala powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
Salt to taste
2 tbsp butter
1/4 cup heavy cream
Chopped coriander leaves for garnishing
Instructions:

1. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mushy.
4. Add cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
5. Allow the mixture to cool, then blend it into a smooth paste in a blender.
6. In the same pan, add chicken pieces and cook until they are slightly browned.
7. Add the blended masala paste to the pan and cook for 10-12 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
8. Add heavy cream and mix well. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
