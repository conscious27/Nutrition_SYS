Ingredients:

4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
1 cup cauliflower florets, boiled and mashed
1/2 cup green peas, boiled and mashed
2 tbsp oil
1 tbsp butter
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 large onion, finely chopped
2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp turmeric powder
1 tbsp pav bhaji masala
Salt to taste
4 pav buns
Butter for toasting
Chopped onion and coriander leaves for garnishing
Instructions:

1. Heat oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat.
2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
6. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, pav bhaji masala, and salt. Mix well.
7. Add mashed potatoes, cauliflower, and green peas. Mix well.
8. Add water as needed to adjust the consistency and let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes.
9. Meanwhile, slice the pav buns in half and butter them on the inside.
10. Toast the buns on a griddle until golden brown.
11. Serve the bhaji hot with toasted pav buns, garnished with chopped onion and coriander leaves.
