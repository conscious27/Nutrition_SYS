Ingredients:

2 cups flattened rice (poha)
1 onion, chopped
1 green chili, chopped
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
Salt to taste
A handful of peanuts
1 tbsp oil
A few curry leaves
1/2 lemon, juiced
Chopped coriander leaves for garnishing
Instructions:

1. Rinse the poha in running water until it softens. Drain off the water and set aside.
2. In a pan, heat the oil and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. When they start to splutter, add curry leaves, onions and green chili. Saute until the onions become translucent.
3. Add turmeric powder and salt and mix well.
4. Add the poha to the pan and mix well until it is coated with the spices. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
5. Add a handful of peanuts and mix well.
6. Turn off the heat and add lemon juice. Mix well.
7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
