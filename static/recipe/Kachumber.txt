Ingredients:

2 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped
1 medium-sized onion, chopped
1 medium-sized cucumber, chopped
1 green chili, finely chopped
1 tbsp lemon juice
Salt to taste
1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves

Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, add the chopped tomatoes, onion, and cucumber.
2. Add the finely chopped green chili to the bowl.
3. Sprinkle salt to taste and add the lemon juice.
4. Mix all the ingredients well.
5. Finally, add the chopped coriander leaves and give it a final mix.
6. Serve the kachumber salad chilled.
