Ingredients:

2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup vegetable oil
Water, as needed
Oil, for deep frying
Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, add the all-purpose flour, salt, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix well.
2. Add the plain yogurt and vegetable oil to the mixing bowl. Mix well.
3. Slowly add water to the flour mixture while kneading the dough. Knead for 5-7 minutes until it forms a smooth and pliable dough.
4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 2-3 hours.
5. After 2-3 hours, divide the dough into equal-sized balls.
6. Roll out each ball into a small disc using a rolling pin.
7. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
8. Once the oil is hot, gently slide the bhatura into the oil and fry until it becomes puffy and golden brown on both sides.
9. Drain the excess oil on a paper towel.
10. Serve hot with chole or chana masala.
