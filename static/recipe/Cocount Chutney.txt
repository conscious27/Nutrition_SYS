Ingredients:

1 cup grated coconut
1 green chili, chopped
1/4 cup roasted chana dal (split Bengal gram)
1/2 inch piece of ginger, peeled and chopped
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp vegetable oil
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp urad dal (split black gram)
1 dried red chili
6-7 curry leaves

Instructions:

1. In a blender or food processor, combine the grated coconut, chopped green chili, roasted chana dal, chopped ginger, salt, and sugar. Blend to a fine paste, adding water as needed to get the desired consistency.
2. Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
3. In a small pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. 4. Add the mustard seeds and urad dal, and let them splutter. Add the dried red chili and curry leaves, and fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
5. Pour the tempering over the chutney and mix well.
6. Serve with dosa, idli, or any other South Indian dish of your choice.
