Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice
2 cups water
3 eggs, hard-boiled and peeled
1 onion, sliced
1 tomato, chopped
1 green chili, slit
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
2 tbsp oil or ghee
Salt to taste
Cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Rinse the rice and soak it in water for 20-30 minutes.
Hard boil the eggs, peel and keep them aside.
2. In a pan, heat oil or ghee over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add sliced onions and fry till they turn golden brown.
4. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chili. Fry for 1-2 minutes.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and fry till they turn soft and mushy.
6. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Fry for a minute.
7. Add soaked rice and fry for 2-3 minutes.
8. Add 2 cups of water and salt to taste. Mix well and bring it to a boil.
9. Cover the pan with a lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is completely absorbed.
10. Once done, switch off the heat and let it rest for 5-10 minutes.
11. Cut the boiled eggs into halves or quarters.
12. Fluff the rice with a fork and add boiled eggs on top.
13. Garnish with cilantro leaves and serve hot.
