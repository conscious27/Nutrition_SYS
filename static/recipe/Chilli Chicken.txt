Ingredients:

500g boneless chicken (cut into small cubes)
2 tbsp corn flour
1 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper powder
1 egg
Oil for deep frying
2 tbsp oil
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 onion (cut into cubes)
2 green chilies (chopped)
1 capsicum (cut into cubes)
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp tomato ketchup
1/2 tsp red chili powder
1/4 tsp black pepper powder
Salt to taste
2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves (for garnishing)
Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, add boneless chicken pieces, corn flour, all-purpose flour, salt, black pepper powder and egg. Mix well to coat chicken with the batter.
2. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken pieces to it and fry until golden brown. Drain and keep aside.
3. In another pan, heat 2 tbsp of oil. Add ginger-garlic paste and saute for a minute.
4. Add onion, green chilies, and capsicum to the pan and saute for 2-3 minutes.
5. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, red chili powder, black pepper powder, and salt to the pan. Mix well.
6. Add fried chicken to the pan and mix well to coat the chicken with the sauce.
7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or noodles.
