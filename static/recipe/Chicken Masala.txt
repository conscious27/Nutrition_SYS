Ingredients:

500g chicken
2 onions, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, finely chopped
1 tbsp ginger paste
1 tbsp garlic paste
2 green chillies, finely chopped
1 tsp red chilli powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp cumin powder
1 tsp garam masala powder
1 tsp turmeric powder
Salt to taste
3 tbsp oil
Coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Fry until golden brown.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chillies. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
4. Add all the spice powders - red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
5. Add the chicken pieces and salt. Mix well and cook for 5-6 minutes.
6. Add 1 cup of water and mix well. Cover the pan and let it cook on medium heat for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked.
7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
