Ingredients:

4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 tablespoon cumin seeds (jeera)
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
Salt to taste
2 tablespoons oil
Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
Instructions:

1. In a pressure cooker, cook the cubed potatoes with water and salt for 1 whistle on medium heat. You can also boil the potatoes in a pot of water on the stove until they are tender.

2. Drain the potatoes and set them aside.

3. In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the cumin seeds and let them splutter.

4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and stir for a few seconds.

5. Add the cooked potatoes to the pan and mix well with the spices. Cook for 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are slightly crispy and golden brown.

6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
