Ingredients:

1 cup dosa batter
1/4 cup finely chopped onions
1/4 cup finely chopped tomatoes
1/4 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
Salt, to taste
Oil, as needed
Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, add dosa batter and salt. Mix well.
2. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle over medium heat.
Grease the tawa with oil.
3. Pour a ladle full of batter on the tawa and spread it in a circular motion to make a thick pancake.
4. Sprinkle a spoonful of chopped onions, tomatoes, and green bell pepper on top of the pancake.
5. Drizzle a little oil around the edges and over the vegetables.
6. Cook the uttapam on medium heat until the bottom turns golden brown and the vegetables on top are cooked.
7. Flip the uttapam and cook for another minute until the other side turns golden brown.
8. Remove the uttapam from the tawa and serve hot with chutney.
