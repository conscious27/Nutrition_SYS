Ingredients:

2 tbsp oil
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1 onion, sliced
1 green bell pepper, sliced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
1 cup cauliflower florets
1 cup broccoli florets
1/2 cup carrot, sliced
1/2 cup green beans, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 cup green peas
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp cumin powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp garam masala powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
Salt to taste
2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves for garnish
Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
2. Add sliced onion and saut√© until golden brown.
3. Add all the sliced bell peppers, cauliflower florets, broccoli florets, sliced carrots, and green beans. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are slightly cooked.
4. Add green peas, chopped tomatoes, and ginger-garlic paste. Mix well and cook for another 5-7 minutes.
Add cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, red 5. chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
6. Once the vegetables are cooked through, turn off the heat.
7 .Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
