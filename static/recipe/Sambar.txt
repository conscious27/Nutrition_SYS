Ingredients:
1 cup toor dal (pigeon pea lentils)
1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, eggplants, drumsticks, okra, potatoes, onions, tomatoes)
1 onion, chopped
2-3 green chilies, slit
1 tbsp tamarind pulp
1 tbsp sambar powder
1 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp fenugreek seeds
2-3 dried red chilies
1 sprig curry leaves
1 tbsp oil
Salt to taste
Instructions:

1. Wash the toor dal in water, add it to a pressure cooker with 3 cups of water and cook for 5-6 whistles.
2. Once the pressure is released, open the lid and mash the dal well. Keep it aside.
3. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute.
4. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté till onions turn golden brown.
5. Add the mixed vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
6. Add turmeric powder, sambar powder, and salt. Mix well.
7. Add 2 cups of water and let it boil for 10-12 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked.
8. Add tamarind pulp and cooked toor dal to the pan. Mix well and let it boil for another 5-7 minutes.
9. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice, dosa, or idli.
