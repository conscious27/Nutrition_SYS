Ingredients:

500 grams of fresh spinach
200 grams of paneer
1 onion, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, finely chopped
2 green chilies, finely chopped
1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste
1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
1 teaspoon of garam masala
1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
1 teaspoon of red chili powder
1 tablespoon of oil
Salt to taste
Water as required
Instructions:

1. Rinse the spinach leaves thoroughly under running water and then chop them roughly.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds to it. Once they start to crackle, add finely chopped onions and green chilies. Saute till onions turn golden brown.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and saute for a minute.
Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for a minute.
5. Add chopped spinach leaves and mix well. Cover and cook on medium flame until spinach is wilted and cooked through.
Once the spinach is cooked, remove it from the flame and let it cool.
6. Transfer the cooked spinach to a blender and blend until it becomes a smooth puree.
7. Heat oil in a pan and add the blended spinach puree to it. Cook on low flame for 3-4 minutes.
8. Add cubed paneer to the pan and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
9. Palak Paneer is ready to be served hot with naan or rice.
