Ingredients:

2 cups plain yogurt
1 medium cucumber, peeled and grated
1 medium tomato, finely chopped
1/2 small red onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp salt
Pinch of black pepper

Instructions:

1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the yogurt, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, and black pepper until smooth.
2. Add the grated cucumber, chopped tomato, chopped onion, and chopped cilantro to the bowl and mix well.
3. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
4. Serve chilled with your favorite dish.
