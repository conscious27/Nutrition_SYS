Ingredients:

500g lamb meat
3-4 tbsp vegetable oil
2-3 cloves
2-3 green cardamom pods
1 black cardamom pod
1 cinnamon stick
1 bay leaf
1 onion, finely chopped
2-3 garlic cloves, minced
2-3 cm ginger, minced
1 tbsp coriander powder
1 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste
1/2 cup yogurt
1/2 cup water
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
2. Add the cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and bay leaf to the pot and fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
3. Add the finely chopped onion to the pot and fry until it turns golden brown.
4. Add the minced garlic and ginger to the pot and fry for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
5. Add the lamb meat to the pot and fry until the meat is browned on all sides.
6. Add the coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, chili powder, paprika, garam masala, and salt to the pot and mix well.
7. Add the yogurt to the pot and mix well to coat the lamb meat.
8. Add 1/2 cup of water to the pot and mix well.
Cover the pot with a lid and let the lamb meat cook on low heat for about 1 hour or until it becomes tender.
9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan bread.
