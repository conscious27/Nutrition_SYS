Ingredients:

2 slices of bread
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
Salt and pepper to taste
Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter or oil.
3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides evenly.
4. Place the bread into the hot pan and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.
5. Serve the egg toast hot with your favorite toppings or as is.
