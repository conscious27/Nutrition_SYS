Ingredients:

200 grams Paneer (Cottage cheese), cut into cubes
1 large Onion, finely chopped
2 Tomatoes, finely chopped
1 Green Bell pepper (Capsicum), chopped
1 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
2 Green Chillies, finely chopped
1 tsp Cumin seeds (Jeera)
1 tsp Coriander seeds (Dhaniya)
1/4 tsp Turmeric powder (Haldi)
1 tsp Kashmiri Red Chilli powder
1 tsp Garam Masala
1 tsp Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves)
2 tbsp Oil
Salt to taste
Coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a kadhai or wok on medium flame.
2. Add cumin seeds and coriander seeds, let them splutter for few seconds.
3. Add chopped onions and green chillies, sauté until onions turn translucent.
4. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté for few more seconds.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
6. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and garam masala, sauté for a minute.
7. Add chopped green bell pepper and cook for few minutes until it becomes tender.
8. Add paneer cubes and kasuri methi, mix everything gently.
9. Cook on low flame for 5-6 minutes until the masala gets coated well on paneer cubes.
10. Add salt to taste and mix well.
11. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or roti.
