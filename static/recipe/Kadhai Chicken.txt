Ingredients:

500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-size pieces
2 onions, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, finely chopped
1 green bell pepper, cut into bite-size pieces
1 red bell pepper, cut into bite-size pieces
2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
1 tablespoon cumin seeds
1 tablespoon coriander seeds
1 tablespoon garam masala powder
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
Salt to taste
Oil for cooking
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
Instructions:

1. In a pan, dry roast cumin seeds and coriander seeds until fragrant. Let them cool and then grind them into a fine powder.
2. Heat oil in a kadhai or a heavy-bottomed pan over medium-high heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell goes away.
4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
5. Add the ground cumin-coriander powder, garam masala powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
6. Add the chicken pieces and mix well until the chicken is coated with the spices. Cook for 5-6 minutes.
7. Add the chopped bell peppers and mix well. Cook for another 5-6 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the peppers are slightly tender.
8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
