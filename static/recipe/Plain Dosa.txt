Ingredients:

1 cup regular or parboiled rice
1/4 cup urad dal
1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
Salt, to taste
Oil, for cooking
Instructions:

1. Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds together in a bowl.
2. Add enough water to cover the mixture and soak for at least 6 hours or overnight.
3. Drain the water and grind the mixture in a blender or wet grinder until smooth.
4. Add water as required to make a smooth batter.
5. Add salt to the batter and mix well. Cover and set aside in a warm place to ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight.
6. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle over medium-high heat.
7. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the hot griddle and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.
8. Drizzle some oil around the edges of the dosa and cook until it turns golden brown and crisp.
9. Flip the dosa over and cook the other side for a few seconds.
10. Remove the dosa from the griddle and serve hot with sambar and chutney.
