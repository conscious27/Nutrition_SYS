Ingredients:

1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp salt
Water, as needed
1 tsp oil
Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, add the whole wheat flour and salt. Mix well.
2. Slowly add water to the flour mixture while kneading the dough. Knead for 5-7 minutes until it forms a smooth and pliable dough.
3. Divide the dough into equal-sized balls.
4. Heat a griddle or a tawa over medium heat.
5. Take one dough ball and flatten it out into a disc using your hands or a rolling pin. Make sure to keep the thickness uniform.
6. Place the flattened disc on the hot griddle or tawa and cook for 1-2 minutes on one side.
7. Flip the bhakri and cook for another 1-2 minutes on the other side.
8. Brush the bhakri with oil and cook for another 1-2 minutes on both sides, until it is crispy and golden brown.
9. Serve hot with your favorite side dish.
