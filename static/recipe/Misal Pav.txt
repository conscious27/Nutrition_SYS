Ingredients:

1 cup mixed sprouts (moong, moth, chana)
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 small tomato, finely chopped
2-3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp ginger paste
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tbsp oil
Salt to taste
Coriander leaves, for garnish
Lemon wedges, for serving
2 pav (bread rolls)
Instructions:

1. Rinse the mixed sprouts and soak them in water for 4-5 hours. Drain the water and keep the sprouts aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. 3. Let them splutter.
4. Add minced garlic, ginger paste, chopped onion, and saut√© until the onion turns translucent.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
6. Add the mixed sprouts and 1 cup of water. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes until the sprouts are tender and the gravy is thick.
7 .Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with pav and lemon wedges.
