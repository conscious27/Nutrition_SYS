Ingredient

Bhindi (Okra), 150g
Onion, 1 medium
Tomatoes, 2 medium
Ginger, 1 inch piece
Garlic, 3 cloves
Green chillies, 2
Turmeric powder, 1/2 tsp
Coriander powder, 1 tsp
Cumin powder, 1/2 tsp
Red chilli powder, 1/2 tsp
Garam masala powder, 1/2 tsp
Salt, to taste
Oil, 1 tbsp

Instructions

1. Wash the bhindi and pat dry with a kitchen towel. Cut off the head and tail of each bhindi and slice them into 1/2 inch pieces.
2. Finely chop the onions and tomatoes. Peel and grate the ginger, and crush the garlic.
3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté till they turn translucent.
4. Add the grated ginger, crushed garlic and green chillies. Sauté for a few seconds.
5. Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chilli powder and salt. Mix well and cook till the tomatoes turn mushy.
6. Add the sliced bhindi and mix well with the masala. Cover and cook for about 10-12 minutes or until the bhindi is cooked and tender.
7. Sprinkle garam masala powder and mix well. Cook for another minute or two.
8. Serve hot with roti or rice.
