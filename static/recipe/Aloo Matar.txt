Ingredients:

2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 cup green peas
1 onion, finely chopped
1 tomato, finely chopped
1 tsp ginger paste
1 tsp garlic paste
2 green chillies, finely chopped
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp red chilli powder
1/2 tsp garam masala powder
Salt to taste
2 tbsp oil
Coriander leaves for garnish
Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they crackle, add chopped onions and green chillies. Sauté until the onions turn translucent.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
3. Add cubed potatoes and green peas to the pan. Mix well.
4. Add all the spices - coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Mix well.
5. Add 1/2 to 1 cup of water, cover the pan, and cook on medium flame until the vegetables are cooked and the water dries up.
6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
