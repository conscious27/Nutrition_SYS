Ingredients:

500 grams fish fillet
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1 tablespoon ginger paste
1 tablespoon garlic paste
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
Salt to taste
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 tablespoon butter
1 onion, chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup tomato puree
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 teaspoon garam masala
Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Cut the fish fillets into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
2. In a bowl, mix together the yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt.
3. Add the fish pieces to the marinade and mix well. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
4. In a pan, heat the oil and butter. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.
5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
6. Add the tomato puree and cook for a few minutes until the mixture thickens.
7. Add the marinated fish to the pan and cook for about 5-6 minutes or until the fish is cooked through.
8. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well.
9. Add the garam masala and mix everything together.
10. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
