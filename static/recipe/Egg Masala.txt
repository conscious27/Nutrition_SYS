Ingredients:

4 eggs
1 onion, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, finely chopped
2 green chilies, finely chopped
1 tsp ginger garlic paste
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp garam masala powder
Salt, to taste
2 tbsp oil
Coriander leaves, for garnishing

Instructions:

1. Boil the eggs and peel them. Prick the eggs with a fork or make 2-3 small slits on each egg.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add the chopped onions. Fry until they turn translucent.
3. Add the green chilies and ginger garlic paste. Fry for a minute or until the raw smell disappears.
4. Add the chopped tomatoes and fry until the oil separates.
5. Add the red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Mix well and fry for a minute.
6. Add 1 cup of water and bring it to a boil.
7. Reduce the heat and add the boiled eggs. Cover and simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the eggs absorb the flavors.
8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
