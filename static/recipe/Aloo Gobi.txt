Ingredients:

2 cups cauliflower florets
2 medium-sized potatoes, cubed
1 medium-sized onion, chopped
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
Salt to taste
2 tbsp oil
1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves for garnish
Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
2. Once the cumin seeds start to splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few seconds.
4. Add cubed potatoes and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
5. Add cauliflower florets, turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
6. Cover the pan and let it cook on low heat for 10-12 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked and tender.
7. Mix well and cook for 2-3 more minutes without the lid.
Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
