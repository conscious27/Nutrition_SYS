Ingredients:

2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp active dry yeast
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup warm water
1/4 cup plain yogurt
2 tbsp melted butter
1/4 cup melted butter for brushing
Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, active dry yeast, sugar, and salt.
2. Add warm water, plain yogurt, and melted butter. Mix well until a soft and sticky dough is formed.
3. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes on a floured surface until it becomes smooth.
Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp 4. cloth, and let it rest for 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.
5. Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C).
6. Divide the dough into 6-8 portions and roll each portion into a round shape, about 1/4 inch thick.
7. Place the naans on a baking sheet and brush them with melted butter.
8. Bake for 5-7 minutes or until they are puffed up and golden brown.
9. Brush with more melted butter and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
