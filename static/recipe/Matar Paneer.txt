Ingredients:

1 cup of paneer, cubed
1 cup of green peas (fresh or frozen)
2-3 tbsp of oil or ghee
1 tsp of cumin seeds
1 bay leaf
1 onion, finely chopped
1 tsp of ginger paste
1 tsp of garlic paste
2-3 tomatoes, finely chopped or pureed
1 tsp of coriander powder
1/2 tsp of turmeric powder
1/2 tsp of red chili powder
1/2 tsp of garam masala powder
Salt, to taste
1-2 tbsp of chopped coriander leaves for garnish
Instructions:

1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds and bay leaf. Fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
2. Add finely chopped onion and fry until it turns golden brown.
3. Add ginger and garlic paste and fry for another minute or until the raw smell goes away.
4. Add chopped tomatoes or tomato puree, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until the oil separates from the masala.
5. Add green peas and paneer cubes to the pan and mix well with the masala. Add a little water if required.
Cover and cook for 8-10 minutes or until the peas are cooked and the paneer becomes soft.
6. Finally, add garam masala powder and chopped coriander leaves. Mix well and turn off the heat.
7. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
