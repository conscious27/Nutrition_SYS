Ingredients:

For dough:

2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ajwain seeds
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup water
For filling:

2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
1/2 cup green peas, boiled
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp fennel seeds
1 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp red chili powder
Salt to taste
Oil for frying
Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, add flour, salt, ajwain seeds, and oil. Mix well.
2. Add water and knead the dough until smooth. Cover the dough and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
3. In a separate bowl, mix together mashed potatoes, green peas, cumin seeds, coriander powder, fennel seeds, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt.
4. Divide the dough into equal parts and roll each part into a ball.
5. Roll out each ball of dough into a thin circle.
6. Cut the circle in half and form a cone shape by joining the two straight edges of the semi-circle together, leaving the curved edge open.
7. Fill the cone with the potato and pea mixture and seal the open edge with a little water.
8. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
Add the samosas to the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
9. Remove from the oil and drain on a paper towel.
10. Serve hot with tamarind chutney or mint chutney.
