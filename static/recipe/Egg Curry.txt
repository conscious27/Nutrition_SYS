Ingredients:

4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
2 onions, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, finely chopped
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
2 green chillies, slit
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp red chilli powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp garam masala powder
Salt, to taste
2 tbsp oil
Coriander leaves, for garnishing

Instructions:

1. Cut the hard-boiled eggs into halves and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle.
3. Add onions and green chillies and sauté until onions turn golden brown.
4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
5. Add tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
6. Add coriander powder, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the spices are cooked.
7. Add 1 cup of water and bring it to a boil.
8. Add the egg halves and let it simmer for 10-12 minutes or until the curry thickens.
9. Add garam masala powder and mix well.
10. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
