Ingredients:

1 cup semolina (sooji)
1/2 onion, chopped
1/2 tomato, chopped
1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, green peas, beans), chopped
1 green chili, chopped
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 inch ginger, grated
1 sprig curry leaves
Salt to taste
2 cups water
2 tbsp oil
Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
3. Add chopped onions, green chili, and ginger. Saute until 4. onions turn translucent.
5. Add chopped vegetables and saute for 2-3 minutes.
6. Add water and salt to the pan and bring it to a boil.
7. Slowly add semolina to the pan while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
8. Reduce the heat to low and cover the pan with a lid. Let it cook for 5-6 minutes.
9. Turn off the heat and let it sit for 2-3 minutes.
10. Fluff up the Upma with a fork and serve hot.
