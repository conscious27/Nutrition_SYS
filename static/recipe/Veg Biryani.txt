Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice
1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes)
1 onion, sliced
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 green chilies, slit
1/2 inch ginger, grated
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 cloves
2 green cardamoms
1 bay leaf
1 cinnamon stick
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp coriander powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
1/4 cup yogurt
2 tbsp oil
Salt to taste
2 cups water

Instructions:

1. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and add the cloves, green cardamoms, bay leaf, and cinnamon stick. Fry for a few seconds.
3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
4. Add ginger, garlic, green chilies, and fry for a minute.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft.
6. Add mixed vegetables, salt, and all the spices (cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder). Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
7. Add yogurt and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
8. Add soaked rice and 2 cups of water. Mix well and bring it to a boil.
9. Reduce heat, cover the pan and let it cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked.
10. Once the rice is cooked, turn off the heat and let it rest for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
