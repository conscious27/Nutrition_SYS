Ingredients:
For the Dosa:

1 cup regular or parboiled rice
1/4 cup urad dal
1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
Salt, to taste
Oil, for cooking
For the Masala:

3-4 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1-2 green chilies, chopped
1 sprig curry leaves
Salt, to taste
1 tbsp oil
Instructions:

1. Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds together in a bowl.
2. Add enough water to cover the mixture and soak for at least 6 hours or overnight.
3. Drain the water and grind the mixture in a blender or wet grinder until smooth.
4. Add water as required to make a smooth batter.
5. Add salt to the batter and mix well. Cover and set aside in a warm place to ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight.
6. To make the masala, heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
Add the mustard seeds and cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
7. Add the chopped onion, green chilies, and curry leaves and saut√© until the onion turns golden brown.
8. Add the mashed potatoes, turmeric powder, and salt and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the masala is heated through.
9. To make the dosa, heat a non-stick tawa or griddle over medium-high heat.
10. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the hot griddle and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.
11. Drizzle some oil around the edges of the dosa and cook until it turns golden brown and crisp.
12. Spread a spoonful of the masala filling in the center of the dosa and fold the dosa in half.
13. Serve hot with sambar and chutney.
