Ingredients:

500g bone-in chicken, cleaned and washed
2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
2 onions, thinly sliced
1/2 cup yogurt
1/2 cup tomato puree
2 tbsp ginger paste
2 tbsp garlic paste
2-3 green chilies, slit
2 bay leaves
4-5 green cardamom pods
2-3 cinnamon sticks
2-3 cloves
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp biryani masala
1/4 cup ghee
2 tbsp vegetable oil
Salt to taste
4 cups water
Instructions:

1. In a large pot, heat ghee and oil on medium-high heat. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
2. Add chicken, ginger paste, garlic paste, and green chilies. Cook for 5-7 minutes until chicken is browned.
3. Add tomato puree, yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, biryani masala, and salt. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes.
4. Add water and bring it to a boil. Reduce heat to low and let it simmer until chicken is cooked through.
5. In a separate pot, bring 8 cups of water to a boil. Add soaked rice, bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, cumin seeds, and salt. Cook for 8-10 minutes until rice is half-cooked.
6. Drain the water and add the rice on top of the chicken curry. Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and the flavors are well combined.
7. Serve hot with raita and salad.
